What is QHHT in Albany, NY?

QHHT (Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique) is a transformative healing modality developed by the late Dolores Cannon over her 45+ year career. Through guided hypnosis, QHHT allows you to access the theta brainwave state—the deepest level of relaxation where profound healing and transformation occur.

As a certified QHHT practitioner in Albany, NY, I guide you through a safe, structured journey to connect with your higher self, explore meaningful inner experiences (sometimes past-life imagery), and receive answers to your most important questions. Many clients experience spontaneous physical healing, emotional release, and profound insights about their life's purpose.

Located at 560 Delaware Ave in Albany's Center Square neighborhood, Clear Path QHHT offers a comfortable, private healing space where you can explore the depths of your consciousness and access your inner wisdom.
